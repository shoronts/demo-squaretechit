Our Design Process

What is your web design process?
How to do you start?

Our main web design process is divided into the following steps:
01

Discovery

Before starting anything, we work with the client to collect detailed information about the business, digital product, its users and explore every aspect of the project.

02

Scope

The steps included in a UX audit will vary depending on the time and budget allotted to the project.

03

Web Structure

We typically start with user personas, key use cases, competitive analysis, moodboards and with a well defined scope we can then start to build sitemaps, information architecture and proceed with wireframes.

04

Design Concept to Layout Design

Now that we have wireframes at hand, we start with content writing for the individual pages with keeping SEO in mind. We start arranging visual elements—like text, images, and shapes along the way.

05

Visual design

Now we focus on the interface of the website keeping visual brand in mind. Typography, iconography, brand color, photography style and art direction will make sure the website looks consistent and visually gorgeous.

06

Site Development

With designs being approved, its time to take those pages and start developing. We mostly start with frond-end (HTML, CSS) then we go through the back-end and so on.

07

Site Testing

Before lunching the site, it will be placed on a internal server where only internal user and anyone who we share the link with can view it. The site testing is cruicial as there will inevitably be issues that need to be addressed before the site goes live.

08

Site Launch

The big day is here! We’ve tested the site, approved by stakeholders and we’re ready to launch. Launching the site is just a start,  we should be prepared for feedback from users to make immediate changes and adjustments.

09

Site Maintenance

Dynamic websites are living, breathing entities that need constant care and maintenance. Updating contents, making changes to the backends and fixing broken links and the list goes on,  are all in a day’s work. All of these phases are cruicial to the Web design process.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to hire your agency?

Our pricing depends on the scope of work, time limit and requirments.

The hourly rates start from $45/per hour for a web designer and go up depending on the level of expertise.

Usually, a 10-15 page website of a tech startup starts from $5,000 and onwards.
Our clients hire us because of our design and development qualities, this allows us to be felixible with any changes and start fast!

All our proposals and offers are based on our hourly rates, even though we work with a fixed budget most of the time.

What type of companies do you work with? Who are your target customers?

Our target customers and companies mostly start-ups, small businesses, non-profits, and enterprise clients across the industries.

How long does it take to successfully complete a website?

The timeline will vary greatly based on the scope of your web design project. Typically, our end-to-end build projects take 2-4 months to complete.

Does your web design company provide website maintenance services?

The launch of a website is just the beginning. Fixing bugs, making new adjustments, design fixes, fixing broken links, editing copy, and making adjustments requires a lot of work. That's why we highly recommend hiring us so we can take care of everything so that you can sleep better at night. We support our clients' projects as long as it seems necessary.

Does your web design agency provide only design or web development as well?

Our team consists of experienced web designers, full-stack developers, QA engineers, and content strategists.
Therefore, we assemble both the design and the development work for our clients.

So, yes, we provide both design and development for a website.

We already showed our web design process. You can read it here (link).

How is your web design agency different from the competitors?

We are a small team of enthusiastic people. We are relentless, we work fast, and we are experts in what we do. From designing to developing websites, we act as a UI/UX design company, branding agency, app developers, web app development agency, and design systems studio. It allows us to provide much more valuable and complete results.

Can you work with my existing Marketing team?

Absolutely. It’s common for our agency to collaborate with digital marketing teams.Throughout the project, we often collaborate with your marketing team to produce new landing pages, website sections, and lead magnets.

Will you be there for me after the website is complete?

Absolutely. We usually provide site maintenance after successfully launching the site. We keep your website up to date, backed-up, secure, fast, and reactive improvements to your website, such as building new landing pages or adding new features if required.

Websites are living, breathing entities that need constant care and maintenance. Updating contents, making changes to the backends, fixing broken links, and the list goes on are all in a day’s work. All of these phases are crucial to the Web design process.
